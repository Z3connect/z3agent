Z3Connect Commission Agent Policy

Complete terms and conditions for our commission-based agent programme

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

Important Notice:

This document outlines the terms, responsibilities, and expectations for commission-based Agents partnering with Us . By applying for, being accepted into, and working within the Z3Connect Commission Agent Programme, Agent's agree to follow this policy completely.

1. Role Description and Responsibilities

Z3Connect commission agents are independent contractors responsible for:

  • Actively promoting and marketing Z3Connect's digital services, which include but are not limited to: websites, web applications, e-commerce platforms, custom software, billing systems, smart billing solutions, management systems, AI chatbots, and online business solutions.
  • Generating qualified leads and identifying potential customers for Z3Connect's services.
  • Effectively communicating the value and benefits of Z3Connect's services to potential clients in a professional manner.
  • Facilitating the sales process by acting as a bridge between Z3Connect and potential clients, including initial consultations and information gathering as guided by Z3Connect.
  • Maintaining a professional and positive representation of the Z3Connect brand at all times.
  • Following all guidelines and training materials provided by Z3Connect thoroughly.

2. Commission Structure and Payment

Commission-Only Position:

This is a commission-based role only. There is no fixed salary, stipend, or guaranteed income.

  • Agents will be compensated solely on a commission basis for each successful sale or client conversion directly attributable to their efforts, as verified by Z3Connect.
  • Specific commission rates (a percentage of the project value or a fixed amount per service) will be clearly communicated to the Agent during the onboarding process and may vary based on the type of service sold and project complexity.
  • A "successful sale" or "client conversion" is defined as a client who signs a service agreement with Z3Connect and makes the initial required payment.
  • Commissions are typically paid out on a monthly basis after project completion and payment receipt.
  • Payments will be made via bank transfer, UPI, or other mutually agreed methods, after deduction of any applicable taxes or fees as required by Indian law.
  • Z3Connect reserves the right to adjust commission structures with 15 days' prior notice to Agents.

3. Training and Resources

  • Z3Connect will provide Agents with necessary training materials, including: service information, sales presentations, FAQs, and guidance documents.
  • Agents are expected to thoroughly review and understand all provided materials before starting promotional activities.
  • Ongoing support and updates on services will be provided by Z3Connect through WhatsApp, email, and video calls as needed.
  • Regular training sessions and updates will be conducted to keep agents informed about new services and best practices.

4. Work Mode and Relationship

Independent Contractor Status:

You are an independent contractor, not an employee of Z3Connect. This is a freelance, part-time, remote opportunity.

  • This agreement does not create an employer-employee, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship between Z3Connect and the Agent.
  • Agents are not bound by fixed working hours and have the flexibility to manage their own schedule and work methods, provided they align with professional standards set by Z3Connect.
  • Agents are responsible for their own tools, equipment (mobile phone, laptop, internet connection), and any expenses incurred in performing their duties unless otherwise agreed in writing by Z3Connect.
  • Agents must have access to reliable internet connectivity and communication tools to effectively carry out their responsibilities.

5. Code of Conduct

Agents are expected to:

  • Represent Z3Connect in a professional, ethical, and honest manner at all times.
  • Provide accurate information about Z3Connect's services and capabilities. Never make false claims, unrealistic guarantees, or misrepresentations.
  • Respect client confidentiality and handle all client information with complete discretion.
  • Not engage in any activity that could harm the reputation or business interests of Z3Connect.
  • Comply with all applicable Indian laws and regulations in their promotional activities.
  • Not use any Z3Connect intellectual property (logos, trademarks, copyrighted material) without prior written consent, except as provided in official marketing materials.
  • Maintain professional communication standards in all interactions with clients and Z3Connect team members.

6. Data Protection and Confidentiality

  • Any client data, internal Z3Connect information, training materials, or proprietary business information shared with the Agent must be treated as strictly confidential.
  • Agents must not disclose, share, or misuse such confidential information for any purpose other than performing their duties under this agreement, either during or after their association with Z3Connect.
  • Agents must comply with all applicable Indian data protection laws regarding any personal data they collect or process on behalf of Z3Connect.
  • Upon termination of this agreement, agents must delete or return all confidential information and materials.

7. Performance and Review

  • While there are no fixed sales quotas, Agents are encouraged to be proactive and consistent in their efforts.
  • Performance may be periodically reviewed based on activity level, lead quality, and successful conversions.
  • Z3Connect may offer incentives, bonuses, or recognition for exceptional performance at its discretion.
  • A certificate of experience may be provided after 6 months of active engagement and satisfactory performance, at Z3Connect's discretion.
  • Top-performing agents may be considered for advanced roles or additional responsibilities.

8. Termination of Agreement

  • Either the Agent or Z3Connect may terminate this agreement with 7 days' written notice via WhatsApp or email.
  • Z3Connect reserves the right to immediately terminate the agreement and an Agent's status for:
    • Breach of any terms of this Agent Policy or the Code of Conduct.
    • Engaging in fraudulent activities, misrepresentation, or unethical practices.
    • Misuse of confidential information or Z3Connect's intellectual property.
    • Prolonged inactivity (more than 30 days without any activity) or consistent underperformance, as determined by Z3Connect after due communication.
  • Upon termination, the Agent must immediately cease all promotional activities for Z3Connect and return any company property or confidential information.
  • Commissions for sales successfully closed and paid for prior to the termination date will be paid according to the agreed schedule, provided they were not a result of activities leading to termination for cause.

9. No Fixed Salary or Employment Benefits

Important Financial Information:

This is a commission-based opportunity with no guaranteed income, salary, or employment benefits.

  • It is explicitly understood that this is a commission-based role only. Agents will not receive any base salary, monthly stipend, or employment benefits (such as health insurance, paid leave, retirement contributions, etc.) from Z3Connect.
  • Agents are solely responsible for their own tax obligations, including income tax, GST (if applicable), and any other statutory filings related to their earnings from commissions as per Indian tax laws.
  • Agents should maintain proper records of their earnings for tax purposes and consult with a tax advisor if needed.

10. Agreement and Acknowledgment

By commencing work as a Z3Connect Commission Agent, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms and conditions outlined in this Z3Connect Commission Agent Policy.
  • You understand that you are an independent contractor and not an employee of Z3Connect.
  • You agree to conduct yourself in a manner consistent with the professional image and values of Z3Connect.
  • You understand the commission-only nature of this opportunity and accept the associated risks and rewards.
  • You have the legal capacity to enter into this agreement and are of at least 18 years of age.

Z3Connect reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. Agents will be notified of any significant changes via WhatsApp or email with reasonable advance notice.
