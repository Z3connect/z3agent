Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Effective Date: May 2025

Last Updated: May 2025

Your Privacy Matters:

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website and apply to become a commission-based agent through our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out the application form or interact with our website, we may collect the following personal information:

Personal Details

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Age and gender
  • City and location

Educational Information

  • Highest qualification
  • Field of study
  • Institution name
  • Year of completion

Professional Details

  • Current occupation
  • Sales experience
  • Available working hours
  • Interest in opportunity

Technical Requirements

  • Device access (smartphone/laptop)
  • Internet connectivity
  • Communication preferences
  • How you heard about us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data you provide to:

  • Process and review your application to become a Z3Connect Commission Agent.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application status, onboarding, and any related queries via WhatsApp, email, or phone calls.
  • Provide you with training materials, guidance, and resources if your application is successful.
  • Administer the Z3Connect Agent Programme effectively.
  • Send updates about our services, policies, or programme changes.
  • Improve our recruitment process and website functionality based on user feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations in India.
  • Maintain records for tax and business compliance purposes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We Do Not Sell Your Data:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your personal data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With authorised internal team members of Z3Connect who are involved in the application review and agent management process, on a need-to-know basis.
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in our operations (such as hosting services, communication platforms), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by Indian law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Z3Connect, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your personal data on secure servers and take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data Retention

  • Kept for business purposes
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Tax and regulatory obligations
  • Secure deletion when not needed

However, no internet-based platform can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by Indian law.

5. Your Rights

Under Indian data protection laws and our commitment to privacy, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

Your Data Rights Include:

  • Access: Request to view the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal limitations.
  • Restriction: Object to or restrict certain processing of your personal data.
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw your application or consent at any time by contacting us.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies (small text files placed on your device) and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience and website functionality.
  • Analyse website traffic and user behaviour patterns.
  • Remember your preferences and improve our services.
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud.

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. If you reject cookies, you may still use our website, but some features may be limited.

7. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement:

Our website and services are intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.

If we become aware that a person under 18 has provided us with personal data without proper consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our systems.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as Google Forms for applications). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make material changes:

  • We will notify you by posting the updated policy on this page.
  • We will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
  • For significant changes, we may notify active agents via WhatsApp or email.
  • We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
